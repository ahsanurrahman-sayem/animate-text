Welcome to Text Motion Studio

A browser-based text animation studio. Create stunning motion graphics right in your browser.

Quick Start:

  • Select a layer and edit text in the Properties panel
  • Drag text on the stage to move it
  • Press Space to play animation
  • Use K to add keyframes
  • Apply presets from the Properties panel
